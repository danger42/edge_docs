For many years, the Apigee Support team has helped hundreds of customers using Apigee Edge for the Private Cloud. The team has gained critical knowledge and key perspectives on common issues that customers face, and the diagnostics that are most useful to troubleshoot these issues.

The Apigee Support team has captured these insights in the Apigee Edge Troubleshooting Guide (PDF) and hope they will help you troubleshoot and resolve issues without having to contact Apigee Support.

While our customer support teams remains available to assist you, this guide will help you to:

  • Determine the source of issues
  • Solve issues independently wherever feasible
  • Perform the relevant diagnostics so that support teams can help resolve issues quicker

To get started, download the Apigee Edge Troubleshooting Guide (PDF).

Who Should Use This Guide

The target audience for this guide comprises developers who are working with Apigee Edge for the Private Cloud release 4.17.01 or higher, as well as support or administration personnel who maintain infrastructure and datastores associated with Edge.

This document is intended for readers with a high-level understanding of Apigee Edge and its architecture, as well as some understanding of basic Edge concepts such as policies, analytics, monetization, and datastores such as Cassandra and Postgres. In addition, it is assumed that the reader is reasonably proficient with the operating system where Edge is installed.

How This Guide Is Organized

The Apigee Edge Troubleshooting Guide (PDF) is divided into four parts:

  • PART 1 - Troubleshooting
    Introduces general debugging techniques such as using trace and debug sessions in Apigee Edge. It also contains procedures to try when you encounter a problem with your APIs at runtime or during deployment, or any problem with analytics, developer portal, monetization, OpenLDAP, or ZooKeeper.
  • PART 2 - Commands Quick Reference
    Provides information about some of the commonly used commands to start and stop Edge components, SQL queries in Postgres, and Cassandra datastores, or get information from ZooKeeper.
  • PART 3 - APIs Quick Reference
    Provides information about some of the commonly used management APIs to get information about Edge entities, servers, or analytics.
  • PART 4 - Properties
    Provides information about some of the important properties that can be configured on Edge components to get desired behavior or optimum performance.
