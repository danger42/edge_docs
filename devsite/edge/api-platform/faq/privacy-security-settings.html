Data Protection Officer and EU Representative

For customers subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Apigee Edge allows you to identify your Data Protection Officer and an EU Representative, including the name and contact information. For more guidance, refer to GDPR Article 27 and Article 37.

To identify a Data Protection Officer or an EU Representative:

  1. Log in to the new Edge UI at https://apigee.com/edge.
  2. Select Admin > Privacy & Security in the left navigation bar.
  3. To identify your Data Protection Officer, enter the name and contact information, and click Update.
  4. To identify your EU Representative, enter the name and contact information, and click Update.
