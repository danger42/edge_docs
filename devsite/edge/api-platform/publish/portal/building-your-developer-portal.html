Note: The developer portal tools described in this section are available in the New Edge experience only.

Use Apigee's simple-to-use, robust portal development tools to build your developer portal, and document and manage access to your APIs, as an alternative to the Drupal-based portal. For a feature comparison, see Feature comparison of the portal development tools.

Use the Apigee self-service portal to:

  • Define your portal content using Markdown or HTML, as shown in the following figure
  • Customize the portal theme using your own style sheet
  • Publish your APIs for consumption by app developers
  • Control who can access the portal content and APIs
  • Customize your domain
  • Extend your portal using scripts
  • Configure your email

For complete details, see Building your portal in the New Edge experience documentation.
